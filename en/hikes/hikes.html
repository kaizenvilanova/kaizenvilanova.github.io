Rutas de montaña por Los Colls y Parc del Foix





In Kaizen Hikes we design mountain routes that delimit Vilanova i la Geltrú and Sitges. In the routes of Los Colls we go around the coast, and in the routes of Parc del Foix we go across the park, direction to Vilafranca del Penedès. All the routes are suitable for people older than 10 years old. The difficulty goes from easy to medium.

Different tours through natural environment of Garraf are from 6km to 30km.

You can choose among more than 20 walks, with different distance and difficulty levels, fitting the rout according to your physical condition.

There are tours in all periods of the year. During the tours, the guide will be explaining the history of the places that are visited, as well as the fauna, the flora, and the geology of the environment.

Hikers will receive a marked map of the rout with interesting places though which the tour will be done.

To obtain information about the available tours, visit our Facebook page: Kaizen Hikes
