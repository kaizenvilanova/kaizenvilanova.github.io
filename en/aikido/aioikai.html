Aioikai and Etsuji Horii Shihan







AIOIKAI is the line of Aikido created by Etsuji Horii Shihan (Osaka, 4th of May, 1964), 7th dan Aikikai.

In 1985 he graduated in Mechanical Engineering at Osaka University and on that same year he joined Aikikai Foundation, where he was as an "uchi-deshi" at Hombu Dojo, Tokyo, with the first Doshu Kisshomaru Ueshiba, the son of Aikido's founder. In 1996, Horii-sensei founded his own dojo in Sanda, Kobe.

Currently, Horii-sensei is Instructor of Aikikai Foundation, director of Aikido Hyogo Prefecture Federation, and he travels around the world teaching Aikido in U.S.A, Spain, France, England, Poland, Bulgaria, Russia, Moldova, China, Philippines, Indonesia, Singapore, Malaysia, Vietnam, Australia, and New Zeland.
