Aikido







Aikido is a martial art created by Morihei Ueshiba. The word Aikido can be translated as "the path of energy and "harmony". The main characteristic of Aikido is to use the other's energy to neutralize the opponent, preferably without hurting, and avoiding the conflict and the aggression. Aikido includes training with and without weapons, focusing on the etiquette, the respect, and the good practices of budo.

The training of Aikido gives balance, flexibility, precision, and coordination, among others. In children, it promotes the respect and the education, while they perform physical activity.

Instructor: Ángel Cortés





He started practising Aikido in Barcelona in 2003.

In 2008, Yamada Shihan 8th Dan Aikikai promoted him to Shodan and in 2013 to Nidan.

In May 2009, he started teaching Aikido in Vilanova i La Geltrú (Barcelona) and founded AikiVilanova. Since September 2016, Ángel has taught Aikido in his own Dojo, Kaizen Dojo, in Vilanova i la Geltrú (Barcelona).

In 2017, Etsuji Horii Shihan 7th Dan Aikikai promoted him to 3rd Dan.

Etsuji Horii, 7th Dan Aikikai is the creator of the line Aioikai. Line and Sensei to which we currently follow. Since 2016, Ángel Cortés has been the official responisble of Aioikai Spain.
