Kaizen Running club is thought to assess all popular runner necessities.
Enrollment fee: 60€ per annum.
The enrollment fee includes:
  • KR official t-shirt.
  • Raffle of dorsals for Mitja de Vilanova.
  • Training proposal of 2/3 days a week.
  • 1 free massage to discharge the legs, releasing muscular tension, and discounts for the following ones.
  • Mountain and road race calendar.
In addition to the club access, we offer different trainings:

1. Personalized training. Fee: 30€ per month.
This option offers:
  • All advantages of taking part in Kaizen Running club.
  • Complete personalized study of the runner condition: pulsing, race rythm, type of race according to the physical condition, aims and follow-up.


2. Personalized training + Massages. Fee: 55€ per month.
This option offers:
  • All advantages of taking part in Kaizen Running club.
  • Complete personalized study of the runner condition: pulsing, race rythm, type of race according to the physical condition, aims and follow-up.
  • 2 free massages every month.

Kaizen Running club will perform two annual races. From November to March, an asphalt race, ending with Barcelona Marathon. From March to November, a mountain race, ending with Montseny Marathon. All these condistions may vary according to the Runner's needs and criteria. Take advantage of these personalized trainings to boost up to your full potential.

The runner is encouraged to design its own calendar of races without being forced to take part in the mountain league or Barcelona Marathon as a final aim.

KR club will try to centralize the training on specific days/times for convenience of all the group members. Besides, runners will be followed periodically in trainings and races to exchange feedback and to verify training is matching targets.

To obtain more information, contact Ángel Cortés: +34 602 24 01 69, kaizendojo.info@gmail.com

Visit our Facebook page Kaizen Running to be updated and do not to miss any races.
