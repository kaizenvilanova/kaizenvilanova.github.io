Kaizen Running club is thought to assess all popular runner necessities. There are three groups or preparations, allowing the Runner to choose the one that best fits to his/her necessities.
Club enrollment. Fee: 60€ per annum.
This option includes:
  • Official shirt of the club.
  • Raffle of numbers for Mitja de Vilanova.
  • Training proposition two days a week.
  • Includes a massage and discounts for the following massages.
  • Optionally, a mountant and road calendar.
In addition to the club access, we offer different trainings:

1. Personalized training. Fee: 30€ per month.
This option offers:
  • All advantages of taking part in Kaizen Running club.
  • Complete personalized study of the runner condition: pulsing, race rythm, type of race according the physical condition, deep follow-up of the Runner and aims.


2. Personalized training + Massages. Fee: 55€ per month.
This option offers:
  • Todas las ventajas de pertenecer al club Kaizen Running.
  • Complete personalized study of the runner condition: pulsing, race rythm, type of race according the physical condition, deep follow-up of the Runner and aims.
  • Two massages every month.

Kaizen Running club will perform two anual race preparation. From November to March, in road, ending with Barcelona Maraton; from March to November, in mountain, ending with Montseny Maraton. All these condistions may vary according to the Runner's necessities and criteria. Take advantage of these personalized trainings to boost up to your maximal potential.

The Runner is encourage to design its own calendar of races without being forced to take part in mountain league or Barcelona Maraton as a final aim.

The club will try to centralize the training in a determined schedule for all the group members. On the other hand, Runners will be followed periodically in trainings and races to exchange opinions and to verify that there is the desired progress.
